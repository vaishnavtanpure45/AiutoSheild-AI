🌐 health-guard-website/

Health Guard Website — Smart Community Health Monitoring

A 4th-semester major project: a web platform that monitors water quality and issues early warnings for water-borne diseases such as cholera, typhoid, and dysentery. Includes a hospital locator, health advisories, government scheme info, an emergency-contact directory, a citizen report form, and an admin panel for data management. Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on a Firebase/MySQL backend.

Team project with Rushikesh Parjane, Sandesh Tadkase, Sarvesh Nirhali, Tejas Satpute & Sahil Ugale · Guided by Prof. Nitin Chopde, Sanjivani University

🦯 mobility-system-pwd/

Mobility System for Persons with Disability

A DTIL design-thinking project under SDG 10 (Reduced Inequalities). Worked through mind mapping, 5W1H, prioritization, SCAMPER, and end-user personas to design an affordable, dual-purpose mobility aid: a walking stick with a spring mechanism that opens into an adjustable, two-legged walker for added stability.

Team project · F.Y. B.Tech CSE, Sanjivani University · Guided by Dr. Ajit Muzumdar & Prof. Pravin Chokakkar
